Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to How2Burn. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you visit our website or use our iOS application and tell you about your privacy rights.

2. Data We Collect

When you use How2Burn, we may collect the following types of data:

  • Health Data: With your explicit permission, we read and write data to Apple Health (HealthKit) such as Active Energy Burned, Basal Energy Burned, Dietary Energy Consumed, and Weight. We only use this data to provide the core functionality of the app.
  • Account Data: If you create an account using Google or Sign in with Apple, we collect your email address and name.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous data regarding how you use the app to help us improve the experience.

3. How We Use HealthKit Data

How2Burn uses HealthKit APIs to read your activity and nutrition data to give you personalized AI insights. We do not use HealthKit data for marketing or advertising purposes. Your health data is never sold to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your account data is securely stored using Firebase infrastructure. We implement robust security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. You can delete your account entirely from within the How2Burn app settings at any time, which will remove your data from our servers.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@how2burn.com.
