Technical Support: How2Burn

Welcome to How2Burn! This step-by-step guide was created to help you make the most of all the app's features and achieve your health, weight loss, and metabolic control goals in a practical and intelligent way.

1. Getting Started and Account Creation

Before you start tracking your goals, you need an approved account.

  • Registration: On the home screen, follow the secure registration flow.

2. Understanding the Dashboard

The Dashboard tab is the heart of the app. It's where you view how your day is going.

  • Overall Caloric Balance: At the top, you'll clearly see the difference between what you've eaten and what you've burned so far.
  • Macronutrients: Track the progress bars for Protein, Carbohydrates, and Fats. The app helps you hit your protein goal without exceeding other macros.
  • WHO Thermometer (Health Limits): How2Burn cares not only about your weight but your long-term health. Check the colored panels to see if your intake of Sugar, Cholesterol, Processed Meats, and Additives/Sweeteners is within World Health Organization limits.
  • Daily Deficit: Shows the "cutoff mark". You'll know exactly how many calories are left (or exceeded) to hit your daily weight loss goal.

3. How to Log Your Meals and Workouts (The + Button)

Our priority is that you spend as little time as possible logging information. That's why we use Artificial Intelligence.

How to log food quickly:

  • Tap the large floating + button (Quick Launch), visible at the bottom of any screen.
  • Select Food and choose the current meal.
  • Choose how you want to input the food:
    • 📸 Camera (via AI): Point at your plate and take a picture. Artificial Intelligence will guess the portions.
    • 🎙️ Voice (via AI): Tap the microphone and speak naturally.
    • 🔎 Barcode: For packaged products, simply scan the barcode.
    • ⌨️ Text/Search: Type manually or search your history.

How to log Workouts and Weight:

  • Workouts: On the + button, tap "Workout". Describe what you did. The AI will use your weight, age, and workout duration to give an exact number of calories burned.
  • Weight: On the + button, tap "Weight" to update the scale.

4. The "Journeys" System (Focus Cycles)

The Journeys tab is for tracking your progress through "sprints" (e.g., a 30- or 60-day diet cycle).

  • Starting a Cycle: On the Journeys screen, click play to start a new cycle.
  • Tracking Results: During the journey, track the graphical comparison:
    • Theoretical Burn: How many pounds you should have lost purely through the math of your daily caloric deficit.
    • Scale Loss: Your actual weight progress over the days.
  • History: When you finish a cycle, it is saved to your archive with in-depth diet reports.

5. Adjusting Your Profile and Biometric Goals

How2Burn is hyper-customizable. Access the Settings tab to align the app with your body.

  • Apple Watch Connection (HealthKit): Enable Health app sync. It's automatic and silent!
  • Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR): In the "Metabolism" section, if you don't use an Apple Watch, tap "Calculate via Biometrics".
  • Setting Your Weight Loss Goal: Slide the control to set how many pounds you want to lose per month.
  • Extra Customization:
    • Change the Language (Portuguese, English, Spanish).
    • Change the weight metric from Kg to Lbs.
    • Manually alter daily caps for Sugar or Cholesterol.
