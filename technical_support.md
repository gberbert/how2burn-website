# Technical Support: How2Burn

Welcome to **How2Burn**! This step-by-step guide was created to help you make the most of all the app's features and achieve your health, weight loss, and metabolic control goals in a practical and intelligent way.

---

## 1. Getting Started and Account Creation
Before you start tracking your goals, you need an approved account.
- **Registration:** On the home screen, follow the secure registration flow.

---

## 2. Understanding the Dashboard
The **Dashboard** tab is the heart of the app. It's where you view how your day is going.
- **Overall Caloric Balance:** At the top, you'll clearly see the difference between what you've eaten and what you've burned so far.
- **Macronutrients:** Track the progress bars for **Protein, Carbohydrates, and Fats**. The app helps you hit your protein goal without exceeding other macros.
- **WHO Thermometer (Health Limits):** How2Burn cares not only about your weight but your long-term health. Check the colored panels to see if your intake of **Sugar, Cholesterol, Processed Meats, and Additives/Sweeteners** is within World Health Organization limits.
- **Daily Deficit:** Shows the "cutoff mark". You'll know exactly how many calories are left (or exceeded) to hit your daily weight loss goal.

---

## 3. How to Log Your Meals and Workouts (The + Button)
Our priority is that you spend as little time as possible logging information. That's why we use Artificial Intelligence.

**How to log food quickly:**
1. Tap the large floating **`+`** button (Quick Launch), visible at the bottom of any screen.
2. Select **Food** and choose the current meal (e.g., Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner).
3. Choose how you want to input the food:
   - 📸 **Camera (via AI):** Point at your plate and take a picture. Artificial Intelligence (Gemini Vision) will read the image, guess the portions, and calculate nutrients for you.
   - 🎙️ **Voice (via AI):** Tap the microphone and speak naturally: *"I ate two rolls with butter and a glass of milk"*. The AI will transform this into data.
   - 🔎 **Barcode:** For packaged products, simply scan the barcode. The app brings up the nutritional table and warns if the product is highly ultra-processed.
   - ⌨️ **Text/Search:** Type manually or search your frequent meals history.

**How to log Workouts and Weight:**
- **Workouts:** On the `+` button, tap "Workout". Describe what you did (e.g., *"30 minutes of swimming"*). The AI will use your weight, age, and workout time to give an exact number of calories burned, without needing external calculators.
- **Weight:** On the `+` button, tap "Weight" to update the scale. Doing this frequently helps the app recalculate your basal burn.

---

## 4. The "Journeys" System (Focus Cycles)
The **Journeys** tab is for tracking your progress through "sprints" (e.g., a 30- or 60-day diet cycle).

- **Starting a Cycle:** On the Journeys screen, click play to start a new cycle. Enter your starting weight. From this moment, a **timer** will run in real-time.
- **Tracking Results:** During the journey, track the graphical comparison:
  - **Theoretical Burn:** How many pounds you should have lost purely through the math of your daily caloric deficit.
  - **Scale Loss:** Your actual weight progress over the days.
- **History:** When you finish a cycle, it is saved to your archive. There you can see impressive accumulated reports, like the total amount of sugar or ultra-processed foods you consumed in the last few months.

---

## 5. Adjusting Your Profile and Biometric Goals
How2Burn is hyper-customizable. Access the **Settings** tab to align the app with your body.

- **Apple Watch Connection (HealthKit):** Enable Health app sync. By doing this, How2Burn will directly pull your basal metabolism calories and daily workouts from your watch. It's automatic and silent!
- **Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR):** In the "Metabolism" section, if you don't use an Apple Watch, tap **"Calculate via Biometrics"**. The app will perfectly calculate your resting calorie burn based on the standard medical formula.
- **Setting Your Weight Loss Goal:** Slide the control to set if you want to lose, for example, `1.5 kg` or `3.0 kg` per month. The app will instantly tell you what your **Daily Caloric Deficit** must be for the math to work out.
- **Extra Customization:**
  - Change the Language (Portuguese, English, Spanish, Chinese, etc.).
  - Change the weight metric from Kg to Lbs.
  - If your nutritionist requested specific limits, you can manually alter the daily caps for **Sugar** or **Cholesterol**.
